Grilled Salmon with Lemon and Herbs

Recipe by: Marlynn Scholand

I chose Grilled Salmon with Lemon and Herbs because it is one of my favorite foods and one of the simplest to prepare. Salmon also contains various essential vitamins and minerals. The recipe was from a website named urbanblisslife.

Steps on how to make Grilled Salmon with Lemon and Herbs:

  1. Step 1: Prepare all the ingredients: Salmon, Lemon, Dill, Salt + Pepper, Melted Butter, and Garlic. (You can use any salmon for this recipe. Heat your outdoor grill to medium heat. Season both sides of the salmon with salt and pepper. Place salmon onto a large sheet of aluminum foil that is large enough to wrap around the salmon. Bring the sides of the foil up to create a barrier so that the next few ingredients won't spill out.
  2. Step 2: Slowly pour melted butter evenly over the salmon.
  3. Step 3: Sprinkle minced garlic and chopped dill evenly over salmon.
  4. Step 4: Place lemon slices over the salmon.
  5. Step 5: Fold up foil edges and corners gently around the salmon. You don't want to pack it in tightly - just lightly so that the entire salmon is covered but has a little bit of breathing room between the meat and the foil. Grill at medium heat for about 15-18 minutes.
  6. Step 6:Ready to Eat!!
